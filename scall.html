Scallion Pancake

Description:

Scallion pancakes, or "cong you bing," are a popular Chinese savory snack made from dough layered with finely chopped scallions. The dough typically consists of flour and water, which is kneaded and rolled out. After spreading a thin layer of oil and sprinkling the scallions on top, the dough is rolled into a log and then coiled into a spiral. This is flattened again and pan-fried until golden and crispy on the outside, while remaining tender inside. The result is a flaky, savory pancake thatâ€™s perfect for dipping in soy sauce or other sauces.

Ingredients:

The dough:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Water
  • Salt

Filling:

  • Scallions
  • Oil (vegetable or sesame)

For frying:

  • More oil for pan frying

How to make:

  1. Mix and rest, and roll out dough
  2. Add the scallions
  3. Fry the pancakes 2-3 minutes per side
  4. Serve
